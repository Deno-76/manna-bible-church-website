“This is the Lord’s doing; it is marvellous in our eyes.” — Psalm 118:23

Our Story

In the year 1997, after completing studies at the Baptist Seminary and while still single, our founder arrived in Mombasa with a burden to teach God's Word faithfully. The Ministry began as the Bible Mania Club, a small outreach dedicated to teaching children the Word of God. Over time, this children's ministry grew into what is now known as Manna Bible Church. Parents were thrilled to see their children learning Scripture, especially through the City Catechism, which became the foundation of the teaching.

Alongside the Children's Ministry, the Church began a Pastor's Training Course every Friday for local men from Likoni who desired to understand the Bible more deeply. Some of these men joined the church family and even became teachers within the training program. The ministry also reached children in the wider community through Vacation Bible School (VBS), held three times a year. During each VBS, the Church compound was filled with the joy and energy of more than 100 children of all ages. They were provided with daily lunch and, more importantly, received faithful Bible teaching that pointed them to Christ.

Since its humble beginnings, Manna Bible Church (MBC) has grown steadily—both in numbers and in spiritual maturity. Though there have been challenges and setbacks along the way, God has preserved and sustained His Church. For this, we give Him all the glory and continue to trust Him for the future.

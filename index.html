What We Believe

We Are a Bible Church; At Manna Bible Church, we are distinguished by our conviction regarding the authority of the Word of God. We believe in the inspiration, infallibility, clarity, and sufficiency of the Scriptures. We also affirm that the Bible is without error in all its parts, in its original form.

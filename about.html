About Manna Bible Church - Likoni

We are a Bible-believing church located in Likoni, passionately serving God and nurturing believers to live out their divine purpose in Christ.

2025 Theme

Advancing the Kingdom of God, Living in Purpose, Power & Unity

Life Mission

To fervently love God and people, expressing this Love frequently and persevering in it forever.

Our Goal

To know Christ, be like Him, and to make Him known.

Chief End

To glorify Yahweh our God and to enjoy Him forever.

Leadership Team

Our leadership team is dedicated to shepherding the congregation in truth, love, and service. Together, they guide the church in fulfilling its mission of advancing God's Kingdom.

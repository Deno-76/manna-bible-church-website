About MST

At the Feet of Jesus School of Theology partners with churches and pastors to to make our vision happen. We divide our curriculum into three levels. Level One covers systematic theology in ten lessons. Level Two provides instruction in Biblical studies and Level Three is comprehensive. We help pastors to have a small library of theology books. To train so many men and women, we have a board of directors, teachers, coordinators, and student leaders with their board members. We have 12 teaching centers in Kenya and one in Tanzania where Pastor Heba is stationed (Tanga).

We thank God for a team of faithful stewards: James, Mwayaa and Jonathan, Pastor Amini, Pastor Dalmas Nzai, Julius and Jefa, Sister Hellen and Robert Opiyo. Our aim is to help pastors to have a profound knowledge of God, and thus our mission is to equip and educate those teaching and edifying the body of Christ. This is our passion, and our pursuit, as we are wholeheartedly committed to developing the minds of men and women. The great commission exhorts us to love the Lord with all our minds. Our heartbeat is for leaders to know the Word in order to impact lives. Welcome to At the Feet of Jesus School of Theology where you can be informed and transformed, to the praise of His glorious grace. "Study to show thyself approved, a workman that needeth not be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." (2 Tim 2:15)

Certificate in Theology

A foundational program designed for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of God’s Word. Focus areas include Bible Interpretation, Church History, and Christian Doctrine.

Short Courses & Workshops

MST offers short-term courses and workshops on Biblical Leadership, Marriage & Family, and Church Administration to empower believers in their callings.

Admission

Enrollment is open to all believers committed to growing in the knowledge of Christ and serving His Church.
